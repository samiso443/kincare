App Details

Schedule

Kincare allows care givers and care recipients to schedule activities including doctor’s visits, medication reminders, procedures and others. The app displays a list of current and upcoming events using color codes and icons to quickly identify their status as well as participants in that activity. For example, a “?” highlights events that have been published to the community of caregivers but have not yet been assigned nor accepted by a specific caregiver. Features include the following:

  • Notifications and alerts to care givers and recipients of upcoming events
  • Simple and intuitive data capture based on type of activity
  • Shared care approach giving the right information to the right participants
  • Security features that allow only certain care providers to view specified data
  • Anonymous cloud access to prevent personal and sensitive information to leak
  • Symptom tracking to better understand results of prescribed medicines and therapies

Quick Details

Using Kincare’s intuitive design users can select the highlighted activity to easily access detailed information about each event. Based on the type of activity, each detailed entry will display the appropriate fields related to that event. For example, doctors’ appointments will include the address for the doctor while scheduled medicine will include prescription name, dosage and other details. Each detailed record will also show the current status for the scheduled activity.

Shared Responsibility

Kincare is built on a foundation of shared care giving where the care recipient, family members and professional caregivers can coordinate the care of a loved one. Whether you simply need to divide the scheduled activities, get help with a difficult task such as changing a bandage or flushing a TPN line, or merely check to see that your loved one has received their medications in a timely manner, Kincare makes sharing the work of caregiving easier and with complete accountability.

Vitals & History

Using Kincare’s Vitals tracking caregivers can enter information on all vitals as well as some common ailments such as chills, constipation, diarrhea, fever, pain and even the care recipient’s mood. For some of these such as mood and pain, the caregiver can also select from a dropdown scale to provide even more detailed information. You can also report on this and other information during specific time segments such as last week, last month, last 3 months and other intervals. Reports can also be filtered in order to select one or more categories.

Logs & Reporting

With Kincare’s built-in reporting caregiver’s no longer need to keep complicated detailed logs on medications, procedures, events, vitals or any other important bit of information. We do it for you. We maintain all of your historical data on every aspect of your health. All you need to do is run an report with a selected time period and with one or more categories of care. We tell you the time, date and all other relevant information for every event, medication, procedure, affliction and others.
